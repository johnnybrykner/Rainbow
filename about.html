Our mission is to promote Polish folk culture and keep our heritage alive.

Patria is a non-profit public organisation. Founded in 2005, it consists of former members of a Polish student folk song and dance ensemble “Katowice”.

Our mission

The idea to form Patria came from the former and current members of “Katowice” during one of the tripa abrod. The goal of the organization was to gather people who share the same passion for Polish folk culture and who's mission is keep it alive.
